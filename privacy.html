Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Scannr is designed with privacy in mind. This Privacy Policy explains how your data is handled.

1. No Data Collection

Scannr does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

The app does NOT:

  • collect personal information
  • track user activity
  • use analytics services
  • display advertisements
  • connect to external servers

2. Local Processing Only

All functionality happens entirely on your device, including:

  • scanning documents
  • compressing images
  • saving files
  • managing folders

Your data never leaves your device.

3. Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

Camera

Used to capture document scans.

Storage / Media Access

Used to save and access scanned files on your device.

These permissions are used strictly for app functionality.

4. File Storage

All files created with Scannr are stored locally on your device. You have full control over your files.

5. No Internet Communication

Scannr does not require an internet connection and does not transmit any data externally.

6. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, security depends on your device’s protection (such as passcode or encryption).

7. Children’s Privacy

Scannr does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children.

8. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated in the future. Updates will be reflected with a new “Last updated” date.
