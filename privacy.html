Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Scannr is designed with privacy in mind. This Privacy Policy explains how your data is handled.

1. Data Collection

Scannr collects minimal data strictly necessary for app functionality. We do not collect personal information such as your name, email, or location.

2. In-App Purchases

Scannr offers in-app purchases managed through RevenueCat. To process purchases, the following data is collected:

  • Purchase history (to unlock and restore premium features)
  • Anonymous device identifier (to link purchases to your device)

This data is transmitted to RevenueCat's servers. RevenueCat acts as our payment processor and does not use your data for advertising or independent purposes. You can review RevenueCat's privacy policy at https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy.

3. Local Processing Only

All core app functionality happens entirely on your device, including:

  • scanning documents
  • compressing images
  • saving files
  • managing folders

Your photos and documents never leave your device.

4. Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

Camera

Used to capture document scans.

Storage / Media Access

Used to save and access files on your device.

Internet

Required solely to process in-app purchases via RevenueCat.

Billing

Required to enable in-app purchases through Google Play.

These permissions are used strictly for app functionality.

5. Third-Party Services

Scannr uses RevenueCat to manage in-app purchases. No other third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services are used.

6. Data Security

Purchase data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS). All other data remains on your device, protected by your device's own security.

7. Data Retention

Anonymous purchase and device data is retained by RevenueCat for as long as necessary to provide purchase functionality. This data is automatically deleted within 90 days of inactivity.

8. Children's Privacy

Scannr does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated in the future. Updates will be reflected with a new "Last updated" date.
